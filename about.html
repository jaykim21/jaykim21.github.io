About Us

Our Story

Our vast team of fishkeeping fanatics works around the clock to bring you the most helpful and accurate aquarium information on the internet.

We are a team of writers from across the world with backgrounds of certified aquarists from hobby fishkeeping and zookeeping.

We cover every topic imaginable in the fishkeeping sphere to provide you with all of the information you need to start your own beautiful underwater world.

Starter Guides

If you are a complete beginner and don’t know your filter from your elbow (that’s a saying, right?), we recommend starting with our starter guides. You will find everything you need to get started.

Aquarium Fish

If you are searching for information on a specific fish or need inspiration for what fish to add to your new aquarium, check out our Freshwater Fish and Saltwater Fish care sheets.

Fish Care

We also cover topics such as diseases and how to cure them, which equipment you should choose for your tank as well as a section on invertebrates, to add some diversity to your aquarium.

Our team is always available to answer any questions you may have. Just reach out to us via the contact form on our website. We LOVE to hear from our readers and encourage you to share your own information and pictures of your tank. (The best will be featured on the site!)

Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced fish fanatic yourself, you are sure to find something interesting here to dive into.

Lastly, we would like to welcome you to the family and we hope you enjoy your time here on Fishkeeping Advice.

Happy Fishkeeping!
